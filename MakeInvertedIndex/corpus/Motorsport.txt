motorsport motorsport or motorsports is a global term used to encompass the group of competitive events which primarily involve the use of motorised vehicles whether for racing or non-racing competition the terminology can also be used to describe forms of competition of two-wheeled motorised vehicles under the banner of motorcycle racing and includes off-road racing such as motocross four- or more wheeled motorsport competition is globally governed by the fédération internationale de lautomobile fia and the fédération internationale de motocyclisme fim governs two-wheeled
