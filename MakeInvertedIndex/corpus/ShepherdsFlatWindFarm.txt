shepherds flat wind farm the shepherds flat wind farm is an 845 megawatt mw wind farm in the us state of oregon the facility is located in eastern oregon in both morrow and gilliam counties near arlington approved in 2008 by state regulators groundbreaking came in 2009 the wind farm was built by caithness energy using general electric ge 2.5 mw wind turbines and it supplies electricity to southern california edison the wind farm is estimated to have an economic impact of $16million annually for oregon it is one of the largest land-based wind farms in the world it officially opened in
