construction the divisions are subdivided into sections each one addressing a narrow scope of the construction work for instance firestopping is addressed in section 078400 - firestopping it forms part of division 07 which is thermal and moisture protection division 07 also addresses building envelope and fireproofing work each section is subdivided into three distinct parts general products
