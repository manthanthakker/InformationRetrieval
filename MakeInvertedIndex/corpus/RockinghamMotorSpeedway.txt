infamous turn 1 and gracelands with a lower speed highly technical infield layout average lap speeds around the circuit range from 1m 24s in the btcc to 1m 12s in british f3 wet handling facilityedit the site has a wet handling area with a kick plate which is used to simulate a variety of driving conditions including ice rain and slippery wet leaves these different road conditions are simulated at much slower speeds than in normal driving and in a fully controlled environment the area is designed to give drivers a greater understanding of the latest abs traction control and crash mitigating technologies and the way that they affect vehicle control historyedit construction of rockingham started late in 1999 with the opening meeting planned for may 2001 4 rockingham motor speedway was
